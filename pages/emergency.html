📋 Your Safety Plan

1. Stay with someone

Don't be alone right now. Call a trusted friend or family member.

2. Remove dangerous items

Move away from anything that could harm you.

3. Ground yourself

Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste.

4. Call a hotline

Talk to someone who understands. Hotlines are free and confidential.

5. Go to the hospital

If you can't keep yourself safe, go to the nearest emergency room.

This information is for emergency situations. If you're in crisis, please reach out for help immediately.
