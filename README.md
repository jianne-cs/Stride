# STRIDE - Mental Health Monster Battle Game

**Live Demo:** https://stride-stress-battle.netlify.app

A gamified mental health web application that helps users visualize and battle their stress, anxiety, and mental health challenges through customizable monster avatars.

## Overview

STRIDE transforms mental wellness into an interactive adventure. Users create personalized monsters representing their stress and anxiety, then battle them using various skills and coping strategies. The app combines gamification with mental health tools to make self-improvement engaging and rewarding.

## Features

### Monster Creation
- Customize monster appearance (shape, eyes, features, color)
- Name your monster and define personal stress triggers
- Track monster HP representing stress levels

### Battle System
- Turn-based combat with skill-based attacks
- Energy management mechanics
- Victory and defeat conditions with progress tracking

### Wellness Tools
- Breathing exercises
- Mood checking
- Grounding techniques
- Journaling prompts

### Additional Features
- Monster diary to track your journey
- Progress tracking and statistics
- Community page to connect with others
- Co-op battle mode for team-based gameplay
- Squad management
- Profile customization

## Tech Stack

- HTML5
- CSS3 (with animations)
- Vanilla JavaScript
- LocalStorage for data persistence

## Getting Started

1. Open the app at https://stride-stress-battle.netlify.app
2. Click "Create Your Monster" to customize your first stress monster
3. Start battling your monsters to reduce stress levels
4. Use quick wellness tools when feeling overwhelmed
